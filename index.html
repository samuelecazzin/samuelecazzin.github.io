About Me

Hello there! I'm Samuele and at this moment I live near Venice. I'am a passionate designer with a love for creating meaningful and user-centric experiences.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with diverse clients and projects, honing my skills in crafting solutions in order to satisfy market and economic needs.

My approach involves gaining a deep understanding of user behavior, a meticulous attention to detail, and a commitment to delivering solutions that align as closely as possible with the company's requirements.

After several years of professional experience, I embarked on a new chapter by enrolling in Start2Impact, a master's program that allowed me to dive into the dynamic realm of UX/UI Design. The digital landscape evolves rapidly, and Start2Impact not only taught me the importance of user-centered design but also instilled in me the significance of staying updated on emerging trends and technologies. In the fast-paced world of digital design, continuous learning is key to creating impactful and innovative solutions.
After completing my high school diploma, I embarked on a transformative journey at SID, an academy where I was immersed in a creative approach. Guided through various projects from brainstorming to showcasing and engaging with real companies, SID offered me a multidisciplinary, three-year experience in design. This unique academy provided the opportunity to challenge myself across diverse design disciplines.
